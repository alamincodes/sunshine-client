03. How do Tokenomics work?

A total 10% tax on every transaction which is subdivided into:

4% weighted distribution to all Spacechimp holders. This is to benefit all long term holders and prevent swing trades, penalize market manipulations.

3% goes into the charity wallet, used only for donations and partnerships to benefit chimps in need.

3% liquidity to the Liquidity Pool on Pancake V2 to ensure price stabilization as market cap increases.

04. Is Liquidity Locked?

75% of the total SOL generated from pre-sale, has been locked for 13.5 months and will be relocked when expired.

05. What does it mean to have ownership?

The ownership of SUNSHINE has been verified, which can be checked on SOL-Scan. The ownership will be renounced once exchange wallet has been excluded from distribution. This is a must if we want to be listed. Many exchanges cannot accept distributions like our holders do.
